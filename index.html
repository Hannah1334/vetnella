> Appearance

While not very muscular, Nella has still grown to be very fit in stature. There isn't very much excess mass to her slender frame, save for her luxuriously rounded bust and rear, allowing her to stay small and agile. While she doesn't have very much strength she makes up for it in endurance and flexibility. Her skin is smooth and blemish-free, stretching perfectly over her hourglass figure in its soft brown hues. Very dark thick brown hair hangs frames her face and reaches all the way to her lower back. Nella's facial structure is rather gentle, her cheekbones visible but softened by rosy rounded cheeks, a small pointed chin, a narrow cute nose, and a pair of full pink lips. Her eyes are wide, being the main cause of her youthful appearance, but her irises are such a dark shade of brown that it is hard to separate them from her black pupils.

> Attire

She is very modest and simple in her style choice, wearing mostly t-shirts and skirts or long pants. Very little jewelry ever decorates her body and her long hair is usually left to hang down naturally.

> Personality

As a human mage trials have led her to be a very calculating individual. She may come off as shy or reserved but Nella is primarily quiet. She continuously watches in order to learn everything she can about a person. When she does speak, her words are few and deliver exactly what is on her mind and nothing more or less. She often spends her energy learning what she can do to help others around her, but some suspect a scheme behind her innocent eyes.

> Nella's Story Thus Far

For her entire childhood, Nella Maria Voltolini was raised in a little orphanage off the city known as Verona, Italy. Unbeknownst to her, she was one of two Voltolini daughters, her father a renown surgeon and her mother an elite violinist. She grew up very shy in a dog-eat-dog world, her only friends being a boy named Apollo and her own violin. Music was her light in a dark world and she pursued it passionately since the day she could walk.

When she came of age and was legally allowed to leave the orphanage to live on her own, Nella headed straight for The Temple of Symphony in Venice, the patron goddess of music. There she would serve her favorite and most cherished deity with her natural talent. A few years brought her to high ranks within the temple, which meant she was allowed around some of the most prestigious musicians of her time. It was with them that she discovered magic!

The Arcane Arts piqued the young woman's interest, and rumors of magical academies made her fantasies possible. She set out in search of a very specific school--proof of its existence nothing more than rumor--for mages with Symphony's blessing, promising to return and continue serving Her. The journey was fruitful and she began learning how to access her own mana.

A short time after her arrival and her developing relationship with an Auror named Ryan O'Connor, her loyalty to her heart led her to run away from the Academy with him. He and his closest friends had committed crimes against the staff of the academy and were considered exiles. While they attempted to live on the run, they were aprehended, and a scuffle resulted in her own death as well as the death of her dearest. Her body rests somewhere in the grasp of the mages they so desperately attempted to escape, her spirit awaiting revival anxiously so she can be with her loved ones again, and continue her journey to becoming a powerful mage.

> Foundations & Relations

The people in Nella's life she's grown close to, through the development of her story. Currently the most important person in her life is Ryan O'Connor, her boyfriend and steady partner.

> Paige Spells

Grabby Hand (Taught by Ryan O'Connor)
Nella summons a strange, sort of ethereal glove that covers her hand, with a magical tether attached. Nella can shoot this glove off of her hand to reach for an object/person a max distance of (15) feet away; and can either haul the object toward her or pull herself toward the object, akin to a grappling hook.

Basic Tool; Violinist's Bow
An intricate violin bow that has the ability to convert into an archer's bow, and has the ability to act like a typical version of both modes. It's only tin in durability and has a magical ability only accessible in archer's bow form (since the transformation has no use and is just for aesthetic sake so she can play her violin with it, goes along with her theme) which allows it to fire an arrow shaped energy construct. Only one of these energy arrows can be active at a time, and are the same durability/grade as the bow itself. In order to form one the caster must pull back on the bowstring, aim, and charge for a rotation. The energy arrows can fly about as fast as an underhanded tossed ball and continue their flight path for thirty feet before dissipating entirely. Regular base nonmagic arrows can still be used with this bow.

Mask of Anonymity (Unpurchased)
A tin construct that covers the entirety of the caster's face and offers her a slight buff in physical speed (x1.25) when summoned (wears off when desummoned, destroyed, or is no longer in physical contact with the caster). The mask has lenses for the eyes that allow the caster to see out but not those on the outside to see in. There are very thin layered vents in the mask's surface that allow the caster to breathe when it is summoned, but are too small for even most insects to crawl in through. While the mask can be removed at will when summoned, it has a magical ability that allows it to stick to the frame of the caster's face perfectly and tightly enough to stay on unless intentionally removed.
